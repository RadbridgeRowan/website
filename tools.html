Parkbeat

Data-driven insights for parks and open space managers

Parkbeat combines mobile location data and economic modeling to provide park managers with visitor estimates and economic impact analysis for their recreational lands.

Parks and open spaces represent critical recreation infrastructure, yet managers often lack the visitor data needed to demonstrate value, allocate resources, and plan for the future.

With millions of acres of public recreational lands generating significant economic activity, data-driven insights are essential for effective stewardship and advocacy.

Key Features

Visitation Analytics

Analyze visitor patterns across time periods and locations using mobile location data insights.

Economic Modeling

Quantify the economic value and regional impact of recreational activities and visitor spending.

Dynamic Reporting

Generate visualizations and reports to support planning, funding requests, and stakeholder communication.

Climate Data Download

Access NASA CMIP6 climate data for any location in just a few clicks

The Climate Data Download tool provides an intuitive interface to access and download climate projection data from NASA's archive for any location in just a few clicks.

Accessing and utilizing complex climate projection data, such as NASA's NEX-GDDP-CMIP6 archive, can be a significant hurdle for those who need this information for climate-aware decision-making.

Our platform simplifies accessing complex data, enabling better climate-aware decisions for researchers, building energy modelers, HVAC engineers, and planners by delivering ready-to-use datasets.

GSI Impact Calculator

Quantify the multiple benefits of green stormwater infrastructure

The GSI Impact Calculator allows you to rapidly quantify and monetize the multiple benefits, or co-benefits, associated with green stormwater infrastructure (GSI) projects.

Evaluating and communicating the full range of economic, social, and environmental benefits of green stormwater infrastructure (GSI) projects can be complex, hindering effective planning and advocacy.

The GSI Impact Calculator is designed for use early in the GSI planning process to evaluate projects at a city block level, and includes co-benefits that extend beyond typical water quality and quantity outcomes.

Try the Calculator

Key Features

Benefit-Cost Analysis

The calculator evaluates the direct and indirect benefits of GSI, including economic feasibility, by comparing installation and maintenance costs for 44 categories of benefits.

Customizable Inputs

Users can tailor the tool to their project's specific location, size, and design, ensuring accurate results based on geographic context and local climate conditions.

Interactive Visualization

The tool features interactive tables and charts to visually represent the co-benefits impacts of GSI projects, enhancing communication with stakeholders.
