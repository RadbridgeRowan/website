Parkbeat

Data-driven insights for parks and open space managers

Parkbeat harnesses big data to generate actionable insights, dynamic visualizations, and predictive analytics on visitation and economic benefits associated with recreational lands.

Parks and Open Space: Our Recreation Infrastructure

America's open spaces and public lands encompass millions of acres of public lands for recreational use, ranging from urban parks to vast wilderness. These natural environments, critical recreation infrastructure, also provide various vital co-benefits and ecosystem services like water supply and air quality.

Challenges for Parks and Open Space Managers

Data is key for park and open space managers, but it also poses challenges when it comes to quantifying and understanding recreational use of their lands and the economic impact of visitation. Parkbeat technology helps park managers deserve a more sophisticated toolkit.

GSI Impact Calculator

Quantify the multiple benefits of green stormwater infrastructure

A powerful web application developed in partnership with The Nature Conservancy to evaluate the economic, social, and environmental impacts of green stormwater infrastructure projects.

Built by Radbridge for The Nature Conservancy in Pennsylvania, this interactive calculator helps stakeholders quantify and monetize the multiple benefits of GSI projects at the block level. Based on research funded by the Water Research Foundation, the tool provides credible information for planning and advocacy.

Visit the calculator at app.gsiimpacthub.org or learn more at gsiimpacthub.org.

Comprehensive Benefit Analysis

The calculator covers 12 major categories of benefits with over 40 subcategories, providing a holistic view of GSI impact:

Early Planning Support

Designed for use early in the GSI planning process, the calculator helps evaluate projects at a city block level or multiple block scale, providing rapid but defensible benefit calculations with minimal user inputs.

Customizable Analysis

Users can input specific data and customize scenarios to reflect local conditions, enabling informed decision-making for GSI implementation in their communities.
